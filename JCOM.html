This class helped me learn a lot about design and how to see the world with a creative lens. I was able to explore adobe products and teach myself things I never knew how to use before.
Assignment: Event Poster
____________
For this assignment we were meant to come up with any kind of event. I chose to do an event that would happen on campus for line dancing. The envision I had was capturing boots dancing, like they have minds of their own. I wanted to give it a magical feel that made people feel like if they went to this event they could dance as freely as the boots. I hoped that if posters like these got put up around campus they would be able to stand out in the overwhelming crowd of posters and hopefully catch peoples eyes.
Assignment: Campaign Project
____________
For our final project we made a campaign for our company of choice. I chose Prismacolors which is a company that sells art supplies, but most known for their colored pencils. In these print ads, I personally drew parts of the design to make the posters feel more hand crafted combining mixed media by putting together traditional art with digital media. The goal of the print ads is to make people feel that if they used prismacolors they’re art can come to life, through creativity and skill.
Part of this assignment was also picking two other ways to execute our ad. I chose to do a bus design and use Prisacolor's preexisting webpage to make a digital community.
For the bus, I’d hope to be able to take advantage of the city life and have multiple buses with this design so the world could see what Prismacolor has to offer outside of the digital realm.
